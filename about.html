Behind The Lens

Meet Shubham

Capturing the soul, not just the smile.

Hello! I'm Shubham, the lead photographer and founder of SP Photography. Based in the heart of Maharashtra, my mission is to freeze your most precious moments into timeless pieces of art.

With a background in both editorial fashion and intimate storytelling, I don't just take pictures—I document feelings. Whether it's the chaotic joy of an Indian wedding, the quiet glow of a maternity shoot, or the high energy of a fashion set, I believe every frame should tell a powerful story.

When I'm not behind the camera, I am constantly chasing the perfect light and dreaming up new concepts for my clients. Let's create a memory together.
